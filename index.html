How it works

Show up. Play. That's it.

Rotating clinics
Weekly sessions cycle through Omaha, Lincoln, Columbus, Kearney, and more on a four-week schedule.
No roster required
Teams form on the day from whoever shows up — colored pinnies, no politics, no commitment.
Jamboree showcases
Six biannual events bring the statewide community together for a full day of competitive play.
Why we exist

A parent once pointed out a kid who had almost gone to a party instead of staying for an open gym. He found his place in volleyball that night. We drove home asking: how many Nebraska boys never even get that invitation?

NBV exists to make sure they do. We're an independent nonprofit — no club affiliation, no fees, no barriers. Just volleyball, open to everyone.
