Nebraska Volleyball Development Association

Growing boys volleyball across Nebraska: one gym at a time.

Nebraska Volleyball Development Association (NBV) is a Nebraska nonprofit dedicated to expanding access to boys volleyball statewide. We run a traveling clinic series, open gyms, and jamboree showcase events for youth players of all skill levels — with a focus on beginners and underserved communities.

No tryouts, no rosters, no experience required.
