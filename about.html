Our mission

Why we do this

Nebraska Volleyball Development Association was founded to answer a simple question: how many Nebraska boys never get an invitation to play volleyball?

Boys volleyball is one of the fastest-growing sports in the country. Interest in Nebraska grows every year. But access isn't equal — not every community has a club, a coach, or even a gym that's open to beginners.

NBV exists to close that gap. We're an independent nonprofit — no club affiliation, no tryouts. Our traveling clinic series, open gyms, and jamboree showcase events bring structured, beginner-friendly programming directly to communities across the state.

Ways to help

There are a lot of ways to make a difference — pick what works for you.

Sponsor an event
Cover clinic or jamboree costs so players never see a fee at the door.
Donate equipment
Volleyballs, nets, and training gear go directly to player development.
Volunteer
Help run events, coach drills, take photos, or handle logistics on game day.
Become a supporter
Join as a founding individual supporter or set up a voluntary family membership.
