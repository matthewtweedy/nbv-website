About Us

Nebraska Volleyball Development Association was founded to answer a simple question: how many Nebraska boys never get an invitation to play volleyball?

We exist to make sure more kids get that invitation. Our traveling clinic series brings structured, beginner-friendly programming to communities across the state.

NBV is an independent nonprofit. We're not affiliated with any club or regional structure. Our goal is simple: get more boys in the gym.
